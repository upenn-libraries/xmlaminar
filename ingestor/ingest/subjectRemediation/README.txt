The subject remediation data structure is built from preferred/
alternate headings extracted from an rdfxml representation of 
of authorized LC Subject Headings. Instructions for downloading
the source xml are below. 

Download skos rdfxml "LC Subject Headings (SKOS/RDF only)" at:
http://id.loc.gov/download/

Direct download link at time of writing (Dec. 19, 2011) is:
http://id.loc.gov/static/data/authoritiessubjects.rdfxml.skos.zip

exclusions.txt contains alternate-preferred subject pairs that 
should *not* be transformed as part of the subject remediation
process.
